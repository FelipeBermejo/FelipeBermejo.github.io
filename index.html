XO Rivals

XO Rivals is an unattended multiplayer in which your goal will be to win a tic-tac-toa match against your rival. However, attaining victory won't be that easy! In this game there's an special twist: in order to leave a mark (an X or an O) you will have to play and beat a videogame. Are you ready for some fun competition? Play it now!

Stealth Project

Have you ever wanted to feel like a spy? To feel the tension of escaping a place with no evidence? Well, this game is a perfect one for you. Stealth Project is a stealth virtual reality game in which you will have to escape a futuristic prision avoiding the patrol robots. A very inmersive experience!

Karlstad University Canvas Redesign

One of the projects I did back on Karlstad University was a Canvas webpage redesign. Canvas is the university's virtual classroom. The project was made to improve the interaction and the ease of use of the interface for both the teachers and the students, taking into account principles of interaction design. Once finished, the redesign was shown and proposed to the web team at Karlstad University.
